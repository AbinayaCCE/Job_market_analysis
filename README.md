CareerInsight Pro
CareerInsight Pro is an innovative web-based platform designed to provide comprehensive job market analysis and insights. By integrating real-time data from Glassdoor, CareerInsight Pro helps job seekers, professionals, and companies make informed decisions about careers, skills, and employment opportunities.

Features
Job Market Dashboard: View real-time data on job postings, including the number of postings, required skills, and years of experience for various job titles across different industries and locations.
Skills Analysis: Analyze the demand for specific skills over time, track skill importance, and identify emerging trends to stay competitive in the job market.
Company Insights: Explore detailed profiles of companies, including job posting counts, required experience levels, company size, and industry categorization.
